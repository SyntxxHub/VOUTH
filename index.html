ABOUT VOUTH

VOUTH is a youth-led initiative dedicated to empowering young people and promoting civic engagement through comprehensive voter education programs.

We believe that an informed and engaged youth population is essential for a thriving democracy. Our mission is to provide accessible resources, educational workshops, and community programs that inspire young people to become active participants in shaping their communities and their future.

Through partnerships with schools, community organizations, and local governments, we work to break down barriers to civic participation and ensure every young voice is heard.

Voter Education Clear guides, unbiased resources, and workshops that help young people understand how elections work and how to participate confidently.
Youth Leadership Programs that develop public-speaking skills, community-organizing tools, and leadership opportunities for emerging young change makers.
Community Partnerships Collaborations with schools, local groups, and civic organizations to expand access and bring voter education directly to young communities.
Access & Inclusion Efforts to remove barriers to civic participation so every young voice is welcomed, informed, and included in the democratic process.

GET STARTED TODAY

Ready to make your voice heard? Follow these simple steps to begin your journey as an informed and engaged citizen.

STEP 1

Register to Vote

Check your eligibility and complete your voter registration. It only takes a few minutes and ensures you can participate in upcoming elections.

STEP 2

Stay Informed

Research candidates, understand ballot measures, and educate yourself on the issues that matter most to you and your community.

STEP 3

Make Your Voice Heard

Vote in every election, attend community meetings, and engage with local representatives to ensure your voice is heard.
